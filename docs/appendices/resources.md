## Seminal Papers

### Physical AI & Embodied Intelligence
1. Brooks, R. (1991). *Intelligence without representation.* AI Journal.
2. Pfeifer, R. & Bongard, J. (2006). *How the Body Shapes the Way We Think.*

### Vision-Language-Action Models
3. Brohan, A. et al. (2023). *RT-2: Vision-Language-Action Models Transfer Web Knowledge to Robotic Control.* Google DeepMind.
4. Kim, M. et al. (2024). *OpenVLA: An Open-Source Vision-Language-Action Model.* Stanford.
5. Team, O. (2023). *Octo: An Open-Source Generalist Robot Policy.* UC Berkeley.
6. Black, K. et al. (2024). *π₀: A Vision-Language-Action Flow Model for General Robot Control.*

### Reinforcement Learning for Robotics
7. Miki, T. et al. (2022). *Learning robust perceptive locomotion for quadrupedal robots in the wild.* Science Robotics.
8. Radosavovic, I. et al. (2024). *Real-World Humanoid Locomotion with Reinforcement Learning.*

### Simulation & Sim-to-Real
9. Tobin, J. et al. (2017). *Domain Randomization for Transferring Deep Neural Networks from Simulation to the Real World.*
10. Mittal, M. et al. (2023). *Orbit: A Unified Simulation Framework for Interactive Robot Learning Environments.*
